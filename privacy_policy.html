CoopRacer – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 20, 2025

CoopRacer (“the App”) is developed by Ashutosh Bhardwaj. This privacy policy explains how the App handles user data.

1. Information We Collect

CoopRacer does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). The App does not require account creation and does not access contacts, photos, GPS location, or device files.

2. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain data for the purpose of delivering and optimizing ads, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • Approximate coarse location (if enabled by Google’s ad services)

This collection is controlled entirely by Google, not by CoopRacer. Review Google’s privacy practices here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. In-App Purchases

The App provides an optional one-time purchase to remove ads. Apple may collect anonymous purchase information (e.g., transaction receipts). CoopRacer does not store or transmit purchase-related personal data.

4. Children’s Privacy

CoopRacer is suitable for general audiences but is not specifically directed at children under 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data Security

The App does not store personal information on servers. Any data collected by Google AdMob is protected by Google’s own security systems.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as required. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
